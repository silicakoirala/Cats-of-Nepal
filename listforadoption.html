HOW TO LIST A CAT/KITTEN FOR ADOPTION?
If you are willing to list any cat for adoption, please include the following details:
1. Reasons for why you are giving the cat/kitten away?
Why are you giving this cat for adoption? How did you find the cat?
2. Age and sex details
What is the age the cat?
Cat age guide
Is the cat a male or a female?
Cat sex guide
3. Single picture of the cat/kitten
Proper single picture of the cat/kitten you are listing for adoption
4. Characteristic
(their personality: active/quiet, shy/friendly)
5. Remarks
(any disease/disability)
5. Your details
Your name, age, address and contact number.
